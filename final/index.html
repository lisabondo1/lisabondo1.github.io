About

Engineering Psychology student with great interest in optimizing user experience for a smoother interaction between humans and, in particular, graphical interfaces. Studying at Aalborg University, I enjoy and thrive working project-oriented and in teams. Furthermore, my knowledge spans widely from user testing, programming and statistics to the psychology behind user experience. Equipped with the latest academic knowledge and having years of business experience with web solutions, I have a strong foundation and desire for creating strong usability.

Prior to my university studies, I acquired a private pilot license in 2019. Flying is now a keen interest of mine, requiring full attention and thus clearing my head from long days of studying. I find this activity particularly interesting, as there is always room for improving and perfecting my skills, since weather conditions are never quite as predicted. Similarly, I enjoy being part of an active community, in which experience is heavily shared and appreciated.
